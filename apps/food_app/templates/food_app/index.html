How Does FoodShare Work?

We easily manage to flow of available food to where it's needed the most.

  • FIRST

    Food Items Become Available

    When a supplier such as a restaurant or a caterer has extra food they sign into FoodShare to let us know. This could be perishable or non-perishable food in any amount.

  • SECOND

    Volunteers Arrange Pick Up

    One of the amazing volunteers we work with goes to the supplier to pick up food and save it from becoming unnecessary waste. They deliver it to one of the food banks in our system.

  • THIRD

    Food Banks Receive New Inventory

    Food Banks in our system are have their inventory automatically tracked. When a Food Bank is running low they are moved to the front of the line so can receive items from a volunteer when they need it the most.
